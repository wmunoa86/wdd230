Tuxtla Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

Site Name: Tuxtla Chamber of Commerce

Reason for Selection: This name represents the chamber of commerce for the town of Tuxtla, focusing on promoting local businesses and economic growth.

Site Purpose

The Tuxtla Chamber of Commerce website aims to:

  • Promote local businesses through networking events, member services, and economic development initiatives.
  • Provide the community with information on public policies, local events, and economic opportunities.

Scenarios

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What has been the population growth in the area?

Color Schema

The chosen color scheme reflects the identity of the Tuxtla Chamber of Commerce:

  • Primary Color: #02c39a - Used for headings, navbars, and accents.
  • Secondary Color: #000000 - Used for backgrounds and paragraphs.

Typography

The chosen fonts enhance readability and reflect the professional tone of the Tuxtla Chamber of Commerce:

  • Heading Font: 'Poppins', sans-serif - Used for all headings.
  • Body Font: 'Raleway', sans-serif - Used for all body text.
